About me

I'm Niels, a Technical Cloud & Microsoft 365 consultant with a strong interest in secure architecture, automation and infrastructure design. I enjoy working at the intersection of cloud, security and networking, where governance and technical depth come together.

In my professional role, I design and support secure cloud environments within public sector organizations. Compliance, risk mitigation and structured governance are central to every implementation, especially in semi-government environments where security and reliability are non-negotiable.

Beyond project work, I actively explore Infrastructure as Code (Terraform), cloud networking, Zero Trust principles and automation. I regularly build Proof of Concepts and home lab projects — ranging from cloud deployments to networking and smart home environments — to continuously strengthen my technical foundation.
